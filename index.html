Our Manifesto

We are for the community, by the community.


We value:

  • Comradery over competition
  • Making things happen over making things perfect
  • Building the community over building rivalries
  • Drinking beer over not drinking beer
  • Collaboration over individual ownership

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.


Here are the rules:

  1. One team has the gauntlet at a time
  2. The challenging team must challenge another team to a group activity within one week of getting the gauntlet.
  3. If the gauntlet is not passed within one week, the gauntlet will be randomly assigned to another team.
  4. The challenge must occur within 2 weeks of the challenge date
  5. The gauntlet is passed to the contender when the challenging team and the contender meet
